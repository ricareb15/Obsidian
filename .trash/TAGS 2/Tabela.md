Linguagem: #html
Assunto: #tags 

Estrutura
```gdscript
<!DOCTYPE html>  
<html lang="en">  
	<head>  
		<meta charset="UTF-8">  
		<meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge">  
		<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">  
		<title>Tabela</title>  
	</head>  
	<body>
		<table border="1" align="center">
			<tr align="center">
				<td colspan="3">
					<h1>Eniac</h1>
				</td>
			</tr>
			
			<tr align="center">
				<td>Sala</td>
				<td>Nome</td>
				<td>Idade</td>
			</tr>
			
			<tr align="center">
				<td rowspan="2">13N</td>
				<td>Ricardo</td>
				<td>18</td>
			</tr>
			
			<tr align="center">
				<td>Eduardo</td>
				<td>24</td>
			</tr>
		</table> 
	</body>  
</html> 
```
